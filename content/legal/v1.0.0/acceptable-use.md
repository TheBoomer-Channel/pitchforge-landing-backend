# PitchForge — Acceptable Use Policy

**Version:** 1.0.0 · **Effective date:** 2026-06-09 · **Previous version:** —

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") lists the activities that are **not
permitted** when using PitchForge. It supplements our
[Terms of Service](/legal/terms).

## You may NOT use PitchForge to

### 1. Illegal activity

- Violate any applicable law or regulation (Spanish, EU, US, or your local
  jurisdiction).
- Infringe intellectual property rights (copyright, trademark, patents).
- Distribute malware, ransomware, phishing kits, or exploit code.
- Facilitate fraud, money laundering, or terrorism financing.

### 2. Abuse of the platform

- **Spam or unsolicited bulk communications** generated via our tools.
- **Scraping or automated abuse** of the Service beyond published API rate
  limits.
- **Reverse engineering** the Service, decompiling our code, or extracting
  models (subject to mandatory statutory exceptions).
- **Reselling or sublicensing** the Service without our written permission.
- **Bypassing usage limits** (e.g., creating multiple free accounts to
  circumvent quotas).

### 3. Harmful content

- Generate content that is **defamatory, harassing, hateful, or threatening**.
- Generate content that **exploits minors** (CSAM is reported to NCMEC /
  AEPD / law enforcement immediately).
- Generate content that **incites violence** against individuals or groups.
- Generate content that is **sexually explicit** involving non-consenting
  parties.

### 4. Misuse of AI capabilities

- Attempt to **jailbreak** or bypass our safety filters on LLM providers.
- Generate content designed to **deceive** (deepfakes, fake reviews, fraudulent
  documents).
- Use the Service for **mass disinformation** campaigns.

## What we do when we detect violations

| Severity | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Minor (first offense) | Warning email, 7-day cure period |
| Moderate (repeat / borderline illegal) | Immediate suspension, no refund |
| Severe (illegal, harmful, CSAM) | Termination, law enforcement notification |

We may preserve evidence for law enforcement where required.

## Reporting violations

If you become aware of a violation, please report it to
[email protected] with:

- Your name and contact details.
- A description of the violation.
- URLs, account names, or other identifying information.
- Any supporting evidence.

We acknowledge reports within **48 hours** and aim to resolve within
**7 days**.
