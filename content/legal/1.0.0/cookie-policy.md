# PitchForge — Cookie Policy

**Version:** 1.0.0 · **Effective date:** 2026-06-09 · **Previous version:** —

This Cookie Policy describes the cookies and similar technologies used by
PitchForge. It complements our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy).

## What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device by a website. It helps
the site remember your actions and preferences over time.

## Categories of cookies we use

### 1. Strictly necessary (always on)

These cookies are required for the site to function. They cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| `__session` | Clerk | Authentication session | Session |
| `__client` | Clerk | Authentication helper | Session |
| `consent_v1` | PitchForge | Stores your consent preferences | 1 year |
| `lang` | PitchForge | Remembers your language | 1 year |

### 2. Analytics (opt-in)

Only loaded after you click **Accept all** or enable Analytics in
[Cookie settings](#managing-cookies).

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| `ph_*` | PostHog | Anonymous usage analytics, session replay | 1 year |
| `_pk_id` | Plausible (fallback) | Privacy-friendly page views | 7 days |

We use these to understand which features are used and to improve the
product. **No cross-site tracking, no fingerprinting, no data sale.**

### 3. Marketing (opt-in, off by default)

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_fbp` | Meta (if used) | Measure ad effectiveness | 90 days |
| `_gcl_au` | Google Ads (if used) | Conversion tracking | 90 days |

We currently do **not** run paid marketing campaigns. These cookies would
only be set if a marketing program is launched, and only with your consent.

### 4. Preferences (opt-in)

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| `theme` | PitchForge | Light / dark / system theme | 1 year |
| `sidebar_collapsed` | PitchForge | Sidebar layout | 1 year |

## Managing cookies

You can change your preferences at any time:

- **In the app:** Settings → Privacy → Cookie preferences.
- **In your browser:** Block or delete cookies for `pitchforge.io`.
- **Globally:** Use the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out at
  <https://youradchoices.com>.

Note: blocking strictly necessary cookies will break authentication.

## Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by third parties (Clerk, Stripe, PostHog). Their use is
governed by the privacy policies of those providers. We require all
sub-processors to comply with GDPR.

## Do Not Track (DNT)

We honor the DNT browser signal: if enabled, we will not load analytics
cookies regardless of your consent setting.

## Changes to this Policy

Material changes are notified at least 30 days in advance. A version history
is maintained in our [Legal changelog](https://github.com/TheBoomer-Channel/pitchforge/blob/main/docs/legal/CHANGELOG.md).
